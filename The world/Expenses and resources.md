# Lifestyle
- At the moment you are mostly resting in the Hornstoat’s Rest inn in Lankshorn
- This costs you 2gp per day for room and half-board
- Only characters that play that day mark the gold off

# Party resources
- The players are welcome to come up with a system about how to split shared treasure etc, or just go with what feels right
- Maybe don’t spend ALL of the gold on a big night out…
